Forecasting tools

A single entry point to the live forecasting and monitoring tools. Each opens in a new tab and shows its own “as of” date.

Forecasts and nowcasts

›
US Forecast Workbook Password

The full quarterly US macro forecast: growth accounting to GDP, with prices, labour, rates and asset prices generated by the structural equations. Edit assumptions and the forecast recomputes live. Replaces the Excel book.

Interactive · scenarios saved · us-forecast-workbook.onrender.com
›
US GDP Nowcast

Current-quarter real GDP growth from a dynamic factor model, with the contribution of each data release to the latest move.

Updated weekly · joshua-bailey.github.io/gdp-nowcast
›
Nonfarm Payrolls Nowcast Alpha · in development

Next month’s payrolls print from a six-model ensemble, plus an implied benchmarked figure that adds a modelled QCEW revision. Backtested on true vintages. Still being validated: treat the numbers as indicative, not settled.

Updated ahead of each employment situation · joshua-bailey.github.io/nfp-nowcast
›
FX Forecast Dashboard

EUR/USD, USD/JPY and GBP/USD: the house forecast set against model-implied paths from uncovered interest parity, real-rate parity and carry, so you can see where the view sits relative to the mechanics.

Updated with the forecast round · joshua-bailey.github.io/fx-forecast-dashboard

US Labour Market Dashboard

Standing jobs-day view: the establishment survey, the household survey and JOLTS side by side, so the payrolls headline can be read against hours, participation, and the flow of openings and quits.
